OUR GOAL IS

to help those in need

We want to build strength in our comunity by raising money to keep mental facilites open. We are tired of seeing the neglect some people have to go through because our shelters aren't clean. Mental health services should be free and clean. And the people who work in those areas should recieve higher pay. By raising money as a group we are contributing to this cause.
